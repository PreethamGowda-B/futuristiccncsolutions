About Us

A decade of precision engineering excellence from Bangalore

Engineering the Future, One Precision Part at a Time

Founded over a decade ago in Bangalore, Futuristic CNC Solutions has grown into one of India's most trusted names in CNC machine services, retrofitting, and industrial electronics repair. Our team of certified engineers brings unmatched expertise to every project — from servo drive diagnostics to full CNC retrofitting — ensuring your machines run at peak performance.

We serve clients across Automotive, Aerospace, Manufacturing, and Heavy Engineering sectors, delivering pan-India service with a commitment to genuine spare parts, fast turnaround, and 24/7 support.

Our Services

Comprehensive CNC solutions for every industrial need

⚙

CNC Machine Services

Comprehensive maintenance, repair, and optimization of all CNC machine types

Our expert technicians handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs, ensuring your CNC machines operate at peak efficiency with minimal downtime.

🔧

VMC/HMC/VTL Machine Services

Specialized service for Vertical, Horizontal, and Vertical Turning Lathes

Complete service solutions for VMC, HMC, and VTL machines including spindle repair, axis calibration, and full machine overhaul.

🔄

CNC Retrofitting

Upgrade legacy machines with modern CNC controls and drives

Breathe new life into your existing machinery with our retrofitting services, replacing outdated controls with state-of-the-art CNC systems.

⚡

Servo Drive Repair

Expert diagnosis and repair of servo drive systems

Precision repair of all major servo drive brands including Fanuc, Siemens, Mitsubishi, and more, with full testing before return.

🌀

Spindle Drive Repair

Spindle motor and drive repair for all CNC brands

Complete spindle drive repair services covering motor rewinding, bearing replacement, and drive board repair.

💡

Industrial Electronics Repair

PCB, PLC, and industrial electronics diagnostics and repair

Expert repair of industrial electronics including PCBs, PLCs, HMIs, and power supplies for all major manufacturers.

📦

CNC Spare Parts Supply

Genuine OEM and compatible spare parts for all CNC brands

We supply genuine OEM spare parts and high-quality compatible alternatives for all major CNC brands with fast delivery.

🤖

Automation Support

Industrial automation integration and support services

Complete automation support including PLC programming, SCADA integration, and industrial network setup.
